Create an Account

Step one: sign up https://www.italki.com/home，You need to create an account and complete your profile firstly. Click “SIGN UP” on the top right of italki main page to get started.



You need sign up with an email address or you may quickly sign in with your Facebook account.




Step Two:confirmation Then you will receive a confirmation email from italki and follow the link inside to complete your registration.




Step Three: Then the webpage will jump to the user investigation page and you just need to fill in a few things to get started.




Then complete your profile, including your profile picture and introduction.





Buy italki Credits

italki credits (ITC) are a virtual currency that is used to pay for teachers and tests on italki. ITC are pegged to the US Dollar: 10 ITC=1 US How to purchase ITC a.When logged in, click on the Buy Credits button in the top-right drop down menu b.When signed in, click on the shopping cart icon next to the ITC icon in the left of the page c. Quick Link: https://www.italki.com/purchase Payment Methods Credit Card, Alipay, Paypal and other forms Processing Fee Varies among different payment methods This processing fee is deducted by our payment partners. We recommend purchasing more italki Credits (ITC) at one time to reduce the processing fee.

Find a Teacher

Find a teacher for the language you want to learn. With thousands of teachers, you'll easily be able to find a teacher that fits your needs and learning style. You can log in the dashboard and click “FIND A TEACHER” button to select or follow the Quick Link: https://www.italki.com/teachers.

Professional Teachers and Community Tutors

Search for a Teacher

Enter information in those searching boxes and click “Browse” button to bring up a list of teachers which match your requirements.



Generally, lessons are to be scheduled several days from now. Another option is Instant Tutoring which allows you to hop directly on and starting learning with a teacher right away! Just select "Available Now" in the dropdown menu and get started! You can browse profiles of teachers to select a teacher. Also, you may bookmark your candidates and make decisions later.

Book Lessons

Step one: select a lesson Click on the name or profile picture of a teacher to entry the Teacher Profile page where you can book professional lessons or trial lessons.



There are three types of lessons that are avaible on italki: professional lessons, informal tutoring and trial lessons. You can even book customised professional lessons.


Step Two: book lessons and check out Tutoring fees vary among lesson types, class length and course amount (book course package will get discount). You just need add date/time and choose a communication tool (Skype is frequently used) for your lessons and then check out. Everything is settled now!

italki teachers will respond to your request within 24 hours. If your class request is accepted, you just need to log up Skype at scheduled time and start online learning. Otherwise, you have to book another lesson. All notices will be sent to your e-mailbox.
