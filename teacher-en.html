1. Requirements for Professional Teachers

Language proficiency：native speaker or advanced level (C2) Possess at least one of the following documentations:   Documents showing your experience as a language teacher  Bachelor’s degree in education or above  Teaching certificate (e.g. TESOL)

2. Requirements for Community Tutors

Language proficiency：native speaker or advanced level (C2)  Excellent in language speaking  Enthusiastic in language teaching  No certificate required

3. All Teachers Please do:点

->Communicate with students promptly and attend lessons reliably
->Treat students with professional courtesy at all times
->Maintain your profile, services, and availability to keep it updated and accurate
->Report problems or violations to italki support staff - we're here to help

4. Please don't

->publicly post or request private contact information like Skype or email
->request or accept payment outside of italki's payment system
->use italki as a way of advertising or recruiting for other services
->offer free lessons, except to students you know well

Application

Step 1 complete online teaching application Log into your italki account and click on "I'm interested in teaching" or click on this direct link http://www.italki.com/teacher/apply。

Follow the links to complete your online application after reading the contents.

Step 2 application reviewed Your application will be reviewed by italki within 2 weeks after it is submitted. You will receive an email and a notification once your application has been accepted, then you may complete your information. Any changes you make to your profile photo, video, and other information must be first approved by italki staff before they will be displayed on your profile.

Notice

When completing your information, please include the following:
1. A "display name" that suits you. The online application will ask for y a "display name". This does not need to be your real or legal name, but it should be a name that you expect your students to call you. Acceptable "display names": "Mr. B", "Teacher Marie", "Chen Yifei", "Kathy" Not acceptable: "TOEFL Teacher", "anime451", "English Tutor"
2. A well-chosen profile picture
3. Accurate language levels for what you will teach and what you already speak.
4. A written introduction about yourself and what you can offer to students.
5. A video introduction to yourself and your lessons (you must speak in every language in which you are C2 or "native" level).
6. (For professional teaching applications) documentation of training or experience as a professional educator.
